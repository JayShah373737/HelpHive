    About HelpHive

    HelpHive is a static demo for micro-volunteering produced by AttachToTech. This single-file template is production-ready for demo purposes.

    • Empowering Everyday Heroes: HelpHive connects passionate individuals with meaningful volunteer opportunities — from local clean-ups to community teaching — making it easy to create real impact in your city.
    • Smart & Scalable Platform: Built with modern AI-inspired technology, HelpHive ensures smooth collaboration, transparent task management, and effortless booking for NGOs, volunteers, and organizers.
    • Designed and developed by AttachToTech, HelpHive represents innovation with purpose — a digital initiative to build stronger, more connected communities through technology.
